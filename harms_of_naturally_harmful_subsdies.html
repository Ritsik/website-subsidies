"Naturally harmful" subsidies could have potential impacts on the environment, economy, and future generations, as a result. To understand "why?", and "how?", let's take an example, and delve into this topic. "Governments may look to start mining activities, and dig for resources that could potentially help the future, like oil, and coal.

While the governments intentions might be "good", these activities may cost a lot of money, and the destruction of mother Earth's natural beauty. As a result, future generations may not be able to thrive, possibly leading to a "less developed" market, and a "less developed" mother Earth!
