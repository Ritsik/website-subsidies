We have already covered the different types of subsidies and unpacked the harms that they are/will be bringing to this world if they are not delegated/ used properly. So, if you are a business or individual wondering how you can contribute to helping the responsible usage/delegation of these subsidies, this section is perfect for you!

To start with, it is important to note that there is not only 1 way to stop the harmful impacts that subsidies could cause/are causing. Additionally, it is also important to note down that many actions have already been taken, and working on those existing actions, while initiating new ones, will be the best way to move forward, in my belief. For example, the Inflation Reduction Act (initiated by the USA) is known to be a popular example of people taking action against “naturally harmful” subsidies. It encourages the use of electric vehicles with 14 billion dollars being invested towards batteries and such vehicles, with buyers from regions like Canada, the USA, and Mexico, receiving upto 7,500 dollars tax credits for the purchase of such vehicles.

This initiative has greatly impacted the status of climate change, as these electric vehicles have the power to reduce quite a lot of pollution, and save the environment! However, with such an act, there are limitations, like the tax credits only being limited to certain regions, and not helping the whole world.

Now, this is where we can come in. As businesses and individuals we can advocate for such initiatives and pitch these ideas to governments, who can take further action from there, and discuss how initiatives like these can be put into larger scaled pictures! This can greatly help the world, and save it from the harmful impacts of “naturally harmful” subsidies!
